Our Mission

Our mission is to help music lovers break free from algorithmic loops and take direct control of their music discovery journey.

With VibeCheck, you can search for artists, preview their top tracks, and save your favorites for later - all in one clean, intuitive interface.

How It Works

  1. Search for Artists

    Use the search bar to find your favorite artists or discover new ones.

  2. Explore by Genre

    Browse music by genre to find tracks that match your current mood.

  3. Preview Songs

    Listen to 30-second previews of tracks before saving them.

  4. Save to Favorites

    Build your personal collection of favorite tracks for easy access.
